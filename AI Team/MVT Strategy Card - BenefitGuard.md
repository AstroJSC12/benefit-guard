# MVT STRATEGY CARD

**Target Customer:** Insured American adults who have had a health insurance claim denied and don't know what to do next. Skews toward people with employer-sponsored or marketplace plans, higher care utilization, chronic conditions, or mental health needs. But the appeal rights knowledge gap is universal: 60% of all insured adults don't know they can legally appeal.

**Pain Point Being Addressed:** Claims get denied and people don't know how to fight back. 18% of insured adults experienced a denied claim in the past year. 60% don't know they have legal appeal rights. 86% don't know what government agency to call. Only 29% of denied claim issues get resolved satisfactorily. People give up, pay out of pocket, or go without care they need.

---

## THE LEAD MAGNET

**Name:** The Denied Claim Playbook: How to Appeal Any Health Insurance Denial in 5 Steps

**Format:** PDF guide (12-15 pages) with step-by-step appeal process walkthrough, 3 word-for-word appeal letter templates (for "not medically necessary" denials, "out-of-network" denials, and "not covered" denials), a list of state insurance regulator contacts, and a one-page "Know Your Rights" cheat sheet citing the ACA, No Surprises Act, and Mental Health Parity Act.

**Core Promise:** Your health insurance claim was denied. This playbook gives you the exact 5-step process and word-for-word letter templates to fight it and win, even if you've never filed an appeal in your life.

**Build Time:** 5-6 hours. BenefitGuard's existing knowledge base already contains federal law citations, appeal processes, call scripts, and consumer rights information. This is mostly assembly and design, not research.
