# MVT STRATEGY CARD

**Target Customer:** Insured American adults who don't understand their health insurance — whether they've had a claim denied, received a confusing bill, or simply can't make sense of their coverage. Skews toward people with employer-sponsored or marketplace plans, higher care utilization, chronic conditions, or mental health needs. But the knowledge gap is universal: only 4% of Americans can correctly define basic insurance terms, and 60% don't know they can legally appeal a denied claim.

**Pain Point Being Addressed:** People don't understand their health insurance, and the system is designed to keep it that way. 96% can't define basic terms. 51% report difficulty understanding what their plan covers. When claims get denied (18% per year), 60% don't know they have appeal rights. When bills arrive (62% higher than expected), 32% just pay them to avoid confusion. The root cause is the same: nobody explains this stuff in plain English. BenefitGuard is the AI copilot that does.
